The Healer

Saint Tajammal

Based in Islamabad, Pakistan, Saint Tajammal has devoted his life to the inner sciences of spirit — the quiet art of listening to revelation and directing its healing frequency to those who suffer, wherever they may be in the world.

His practice is anchored in the four revealed books — the Torah, the Zabur, the Injeel, and the Holy Qur'an. The verses themselves are the medicine; the healer is only the vessel through which they travel.

Philosophy

Quantum Entanglement

Modern physics tells us that two particles, once entangled, remain connected across any distance — what happens to one is instantly mirrored in the other. The mystics have always known this. The soul, too, is entangled — with its Source, with the prophets who carried the Word, and with every other awakened heart.

In a remote healing session, no distance is real. A phone call from Islamabad to anywhere on Earth is enough, because the verses do not travel through wires — they travel through the entangled fabric of being.
